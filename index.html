About Us

Orbit children hospital and critical care center is the first dedicated pediatric center of Akola started 10 years back. Under the able leadership of Dr Naresh Bajaj and joint efforts of whole team, it has catered level II services and treated nearly 50,000 pediatric patients till date(including critical neonatal, pediatric and surgical patients).It has made a good will in the minds of not only people from Akola but also nearby districts. Transparency, mutual symbiotic relationship among directors and good nursing care are the main pillars for the decade long success of Orbit hospital.

A Step Forward

Pediatrics is a vast branch that includes not only neonatalogy and general pediatrics but also fetal and adolescents services. Superspeciality is the need of the hour. Let us not restrict it to metropolitan cities. Every needy child has a right and deserves to get the most accurate diagnosis and access to the latest medical developments by the experts in the field.

Overwhelmed with the joy of success, we are taking a step forward. Bajaj hospital is a well known centrally located pediatric hospital near Orbit hospital.Dr Naresh Bajaj has kindly consented to let out the first floor exclusively for Orbit superspeciality clinics. It has been jointly decided by the directors to manage Orbit superspeciality clinics. It has also been agreed to dedicate it in the memory of "NIKUNJ"(s/o Dr Anup Kothari).
